About Chestnut Boarding

Licensed by the Colorado Department of Agriculture (PACFA), we provide an exclusive, boutique boarding experience entirely within our primary residence. By strictly limiting our care to a maximum of three client dogs at any time, we ensure your pet receives the personalized attention, comfort, and direct supervision they crave in a calm, home-centered environment.

Our Philosophy

We believe that boarding shouldn't be a stressful experience for pets or their owners. We treat every guest with the same care, attention, and respect that we give our own pets. From personalized feeding schedules to dedicated playtime, your pet's well-being is our top priority.

Our Facility

Our facility is designed with safety and comfort in mind. We maintain strict hygiene protocols, comfortable environments, and secure play areas with a large back yard. We also require all guests to have up-to-date vaccinations to ensure the health of our entire boarding community.
